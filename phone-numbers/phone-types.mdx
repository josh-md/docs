## Local Numbers

MessageDesk supports local phone numbers:

- **US numbers:** Available in every state
- **Canadian numbers:** Available across all provinces

You can host an existing local phone number or purchase a new phone number through MessageDesk by area code.

## Toll-Free

MessageDesk supports North American toll-free numbers with these prefixes:

- 800
- 833
- 844
- 855
- 866
- 877
- 888

Toll-Free messaging includes all SMS features

## Twilio Integration

Connect your twilio account and any SMS phone numbers to MessageDesk

**What's Required:**

1. [Twilio Account SID](https://help.twilio.com/articles/14726256820123-What-is-a-Twilio-Account-SID-and-where-can-I-find-it-)
2. [Twilio Auth Token](https://help.twilio.com/articles/223136027-Auth-Tokens-and-How-to-Change-Them)
3. 10 Digit Phone Number(s) for Twilio Account
